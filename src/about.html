Who We Are

Kodex Africa is more than an organization; we are a catalyst for change, a beacon of hope, and a driving force behind the technological transformation of Africa. Our identity is rooted in our unwavering commitment to empowering Africa's brightest minds, fostering innovation, and creating opportunities for growth and development. We are a passionate community of educators, technologists, mentors, and visionaries who believe in the potential of Africa's youth. We aim to unlock that potential by providing access to quality mentorship, and resources in the tech sector. Kodex Africa is more than an organization; we are a movement, a collective force that believes in the limitless possibilities of Africa's tech talent. Together, we are shaping the future of technology on the continent and empowering Africa's brightest minds to lead the way.

At Kodex Africa, we are developing a pipeline of African tech talents equipped with the skills, support, and network that they need to solve African problems.
